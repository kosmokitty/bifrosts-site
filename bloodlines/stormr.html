Introduction

Stormr sits at the northwest portion of the island, open to the mountains and the sky. Once proud, secretive, and untouchable — a city that believed itself closest to the gods — it has been brought low by war, famine, and betrayal from within. Eldur has claimed much of their territory. Supplies dwindle. The Monarch, once the city's unbreakable pillar, shattered the trust of their people and was executed. The crown is gone. The pieces remain.
