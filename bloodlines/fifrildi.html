Introduction

Nestled in the curve of a forested peninsula past the Sker Mountains, Fifrildi's placement between where the cities of Laguz and Forseti once dwelled is fitting for the mixture of two cultures it now stands as. Once a diplomatic outpost made necessary by the threat of undead monsters and fiery religious fanatics closing in, it has grown into its own city — one that hosts both blue and green eyes side by side.
