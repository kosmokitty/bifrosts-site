Introduction

Eldur believes themselves to be born from fire — embers sizzling through their veins, phoenix ash burning in their blood. They are a regal people, unshaken in their belief that they stand above the other cities, a conviction proven through conquest. Storm's lands seized, and its Monarchy reduced to memory, burned by the claws of Eldurians.
