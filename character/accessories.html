Accessories

In the rugged world of Fyrstrheim, accessories are more than mere decoration; they are a reflection of a cat's survival and lifestyle. With resources scarce and trade routes perilous, every trinket tells a story of origin, rank, and allegiance.

While Purchased Accessories grant physical stat perks, you are encouraged to personalize your appearance within the bounds of regional lore.

Fifrildi

A blend of forest and coast. If it can be scavenged, it can be worn. Their style is raw, organic, and salvaged.

  • Bleached Bone & Shark Teeth
  • Stained Glass & Polished Stones
  • Iridescent Scales & Pearls
  • Braided Vines & Sturdy Leathers
  • Wooden Charms & Wild Feathers

Stormr

An airy, refined kingdom. They hold onto delicate textiles, silky fabrics, and favor accessories that flow with the breeze.

  • Fine Silks & Vibrant Threads
  • Lightweight Metal Jewelry
  • Pressed Leaves & Highland Plants
  • Fabrics & Feathers

Eldur

A kingdom of fire and industry. They favor permanence and brilliance, using the earth's deep treasures and creations from the forge to show status.

  • Rare Ores & Raw Minerals
  • Polished Gems & Bright Fabrics
  • Heavy Tooled Leathers
  • Protective Metallic Cuffs

Note: No accessory may exceed the size of the King's.
