Aging and Ranking

​In Bifrosts, all characters will age naturally to ensure that we're keeping slots open and keep the worldbuilding realistic. No cat will ever be immortal. Throughout this process, members have the ability to NPC their character. Becoming an NPC means surrendering your character to the group. This fact means that Bifrost's team may choose to kill off your character at any given time, but we will always seek permission beforehand. Characters will be subject to pass via the plague.

Each city has different cultures and ranking requirements that may vary; please see their pages for more in-depth information regarding it. This page will cover aging, ranking, and the requirements as a whole.​​​​

Aging

Kitten

0-5 Cycles

  • Age up three cycles every two weeks.
  • Duration: 4 Weeks

Novice

6-9 Cycles

Once a kitten has reached the age of 6 cycles, they will become a novice and assigned a mentor or teacher for the foreseeable future. There are no requirements for this stage.

  • Age up one cycle every two weeks.
  • Duration: 8 Weeks

Specialised Novice

10-19 Cycles

Once a novice reaches 10 cycles, they will either choose or be given a rank to specialize in; this will be what determines your character's future. All specialized novices must complete an assessment in order to graduate on time.

  • Age up one cycle every two weeks.
  • Duration: 20 Weeks

Adult

20-59 Cycles

Once a character reaches 20 cycles, they will be classed as an official member of the city they reside in. They will be granted access to all areas of the territory without any adult supervision.

  • Age up one cycle every two weeks.
  • Duration: 60 Weeks

Senior Adult

60-69 Cycles

Once a character reaches 60 cycles, they will be classed as a senior adult. They are well-respected and honorable members of the city, having served for endless cycles.

  • Age up one cycle every two weeks.
  • Duration: 20 Weeks

Elder

70-100 Cycles

Once a character reaches the age of 70 cycles, they have the option to retire as an elder. When they hit 80 cycles, their character is forced to retire, and the player may choose if they want their character to become an NPC or still playable on their part.

  • Age up one cycle every two weeks.
  • Duration: 60 Weeks

Ranking

Ranking is one of the fundamental ways for your character to "level up". The older your character becomes, the more you'll be able to access such as weaponry, territory access, involvement in significant plots, and more! That being said, there are no requirements to rank up until your character is nearing 20 cycles, then they'll be required to complete a graduation assessment.

Graduation Assessments

Graduation assessments are a piece of literature or artwork displaying your character completing a "final" request from their mentor or doing something extraordinary. Assessments can span from anything as long as they link to the character's occupation within the city and follow the general worldbuilding.

This must be completed before a character reaches 25 cycles, or the character will pass away the following age-up — this is a 2.5 month grace period to accommodate IRL responsibilities and activity concerns. If you're still unable to complete their assessment by then and don't want to lose your character, please open a ticket and chat with the staff team.​

  • Members on hiatus will not receive a death timer of any kind, but we ask that they complete the assessment as soon as possible once they come off their hiatus.

​Assessments must amount to 90 stardust, which can look like:

Literature

A 1,350 written piece pertaining to the character's assessment.

Artwork

A fullbody with moderate background and moderate shading.
