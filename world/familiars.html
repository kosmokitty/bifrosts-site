Familiars

A familiar is a spiritual extension of one’s soul, manifesting as the form of an animal. It is a familiar’s duty to guide, protect, and assist those they are bound to. In turn, it is an individual’s duty to care for and protect their familiar for as long as they may live. Though it may have an independent life of its own, a familiar remains closely linked to its individual.

Though they take the form of animals, a familiar’s life extends beyond the form that it takes. Familiars live beyond the basic necessities of normal animals, needing not nourishment nor sleep. Their lifespan exceeds the span of their animal form, possessing the capacity to live as long as their mortal link remains in Fyrstrheim. Instead, they age alongside their soul-link, killed only by fatal blows or when their soul-linked individual passes.

A Soulbound Companion

Every new character is given a familiar upon joining the group. Familiars are chosen from animals native to Northern Germanic regions. When your character is created, you’ll receive six randomly selected animal forms to choose from—pick one to become your character’s lifelong companion. If you’d like a different roll, rerolls can be purchased in the shop.

Familiar Bonding and Death

Familiars are soulbound to their character from birth. If your character dies, so does their familiar. However, if a familiar dies first, the soul link is broken permanently—your character will survive, but they can never bond with another familiar. This loss is profound, like losing half their soul.

Familiars age alongside their soul-linked character, regardless of species. They cannot die from age or hunger—only fatal wounds or the death of their companion.

Communication and Behavior

Familiars can understand their bonded character’s words and commands, but they cannot speak in return. Their behavior closely mirrors that of their species. Though they have deep thoughts, familiars rarely approach others, as interacting with another feline’s familiar is seen as deeply intimate and risky.

Being touched by another’s familiar is a rare show of trust.

Familiars do not need to eat, though they can.

Appearance Rules

While familiars may have slight color or design variations, they must clearly resemble their real-world species. Their eyes must match the eye color of their bonded character, symbolizing their soul link. Familiars must be the same gender as the character they’re bonded to.

Size Guidelines

Familiars should be appropriately sized for their species and must not exceed the minimum height requirement of their character’s lineage:

  • Green: 13''
  • Blue: 12''
  • Red: 11''
  • Grey: 10''

Design Guidelines

All familiars must follow Bifrost’s design and naming rules. Please ensure your familiar’s design reflects its species clearly.

Note: If the chosen animal species is typically venomous or poisonous, your familiar will not possess those traits.
