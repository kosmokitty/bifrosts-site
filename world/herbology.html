Herbology

A list of all the herbs that can be found throughout Fyrstrheim, and a page often visited by our city healers. If you have additional herbs you'd like to see on this page, please send a suggestion in our discord.

Common Uncommon Rare Poisonous

🟢 Alpine Bistort

Root treats abscesses, mouth infections, and sore throats. Good for ulcers.

🟡 Angelica

Protects against contagious sickness and lung infections. Strengthens the heart.

🟡 Arnica

Used for bruising and muscle aches. Do not use on open wounds.

🟢 Arctic Thyme

Strengthens heart and helps bronchitis symptoms.

🟢 Beech Leaves

Soothes nerves, stomach inflammation, and burned skin.

🟢 Bilberry

Sharpens senses and cools blood during fever.

🟢 Birch Bark

Ground into paste for skin infections or used for splints.

🟢 Black Alder

Treats bleeding, sore throats, and arthritis swelling.

🟡 Bogbean

Combats flu and treats snake bites or other venoms.

🟢 Borage

Brings down fevers and eases dry coughs.

🟢 Broom

Used for broken legs and serious wound cleaning.

🟢 Burnet

Soothes boils and stops internal bleeding.

🟢 Catchweed

Fuzzy burrs used to keep poultices stuck to fur.

🟣 Catmint

The primary cure for Greencough and Whitecough.

🟢 Chamomile

Calms the heart and mind; soothes joint pain.

🟢 Chives

Expels worms and aids memory and sleep.

🟢 Cinquefoil

Reduces inflammation and aids jaundice.

🟣 Cloudberry

Vital for winter fatigue and scurvy prevention.

🟢 Cobwebs

Pressed over wounds to soak up blood and bind them.

🟢 Comfrey

Repairs broken bones and soothes burns.

🟢 Daisy

Good for aching joints and skin rashes.

🟢 Dandelion

Milk for bee stings; leaves for digestion.

Ghostly white mushroom. Causes total liver and kidney failure. Untreatable.

💀 Deathberries

Red Yew berries. Shuts down the heart almost instantly. Lethal.

🟢 Elderberry

Prevents flu and treats winter chills.

🟢 Feverfew

Best for migraines and cooling high fevers.

💀 Foxglove

Seeds and leaves cause cardiac collapse. Highly poisonous.

🟡 Gentian

Softens muscle spasms and shivers.

🟢 Goldenrod

Treats arthritis and heals skin ulcers.

🟡 Hawkweed

Used for asthma and bronchitis.

🟢 Heather

Soothes stomachs and cleans wounds.

💀 Hemlock

Causes paralysis and respiratory failure. Extremely toxic.

🟢 Horsetail

Stops bleeding and cleans deep infections.

🟢 Icelandic Moss

Coats the throat and stomach to stop irritation.

🟢 Juniper

Berries for digestion, breathing, and kidney issues.

🟡 Kelp

Protects the heart and combats stress.

🟢 Lady's Mantle

Root used to stop heavy bleeding.

🟢 Lovage

Eases stomach pain and eye redness.

🟢 Meadwort

Gentle painkiller for joints and reflux.

🟢 Mint

Reduces stomach inflammation and boosts immunity.

Eases headaches and whooping cough.

🟡 Poppy

🟢 Reindeer Moss
