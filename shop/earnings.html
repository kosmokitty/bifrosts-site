Earnings

In our world, we use the currency of Stardust! You can collect Stardust by drawing, writing, completing prompts, participating in quests, or through interacting with the world! You can use Stardust to purchase plots, litters, character accessories, mutations, additional character coins, tokens, or items for your character!

Here, you can view how much stardust you may earn with your artwork or literature contributions!

Rules

  1. Stardust cannot be transferred between players.
  2. You may not upload pre-existing artwork to earn stardust.
  3. Purchases cannot be split between members and must be paid in full.
  4. Purchases cannot send your bank into the negatives.
  5. We can refuse a purchase-request for any reason.
