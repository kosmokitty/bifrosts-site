Shop

In our world, we use the currency of Stardust! You can collect Stardust by drawing, writing, completing prompts, participating in quests, or through interacting with the world! You can use Stardust to purchase plots, litters, character accessories, mutations, additional character coins, tokens, or items for your character!

You can't make any purchases on our website, but on our Discord you'll find the Claims Channel! This is where you can order items from the shop which will then be placed in your player inventory. Your inventory can be viewed on your character card!

Rules

  1. Stardust may not be transferred between players!
  2. You may not upload pre-existing artwork to earn stardust.
  3. Purchases may not be split between members, it must be paid in full!
  4. Purchases may not send your bank into the negatives.
  5. We may refuse a purchase for any reason.

Outpost Token

This token can be purchased alongside a character rune and is limited to five purchases per season, which lasts two months. It allows an existing member to bring in an orphaned character of any age from a city outpost. However, characters younger than six cycles must have a novice or adult guide, either through a new NPC or by using multiple outpost tokens from other players. Some cities may be unavailable for this token if they lack an outpost or do not need new characters. The token expires at the end of the season, with no refunds or extensions.

Genetic Mutations

All of the items listed below must be discussed in a ticket before purchasing. Some genetic mutations do not take up a ban slot.

Abnormally Small

200 Stardust

This mutation causes the character to be abnormally small, falling outside the usual size guidelines. While there are no health issues associated with this size, they may face limitations in physical abilities like jumping, swimming, or running.

Abnormally Large

200 Stardust

This mutation causes the character to be abnormally large, surpassing the usual size limits. While the character doesn’t face any health issues, being larger may grant advantages in strength and speed, making them physically more capable than their peers.

Melanism

100 Stardust

A genetic condition that leads to an increased amount of melanin in the skin and fur. These characters are entirely black, including their fur, skin, claws, and nose. However, they do not tend to experience health issues because of this trait.

Vitiligo

100 Stardust

A genetic condition that causes patches of skin or fur to lose color, with the areas gradually expanding over time. The condition does not affect the overall health of the character, but the appearance can change significantly.

Four Ears

400 Stardust

A recessive mutation that results in an additional set of ear flaps. While the extra flaps do not provide additional hearing organs, they still alter their appearance. This mutation does not impact the character's hearing abilities.

Polycaudal

300 Stardust

A mutation that results in two tails. While most cats can control both tails simultaneously, they cannot move them independently. Although this trait is seen as unusual, it doesn't present significant disadvantages.

Polydactyl

100 Stardust

Cats with extra digits on one or more paws, which can vary in size and structure. The condition may lead to injuries, infections, or irregular growth of the nails and pads.

Syndactyly

100 Stardust

A condition where cats have fused or abnormal digits, affecting paw pads and nails. Though it causes minimal discomfort, affected cats may experience little to no lameness.

Bans

All items listed below must be discussed in a ticket before purchase. They may be declined or approved to ensure the condition or ailment is portrayed accurately and sensitively, while avoiding harmful stereotypes. Bans take up a slot on your character card, as each player may only own 5 bans at a time, while characters may only have a total of 2 bans applied. Bans typically are limited per city, which can be viewed on our character-cards.

Albinism

300 Stardust

A genetic disorder that results in little or no melanin production, leading to white fur and pink or blue eyes. Cats with albinism may experience vision problems and light sensitivity, with some possibly having a lazy eye.

Munchkin

500 Stardust

A breed characterized by short legs due to a genetic mutation causing dwarfism. Munchkins are at higher risk for osteoarthritis and spinal issues like lordosis, and may struggle participating in physical activites.

Minskin

500 Stardust

A hybrid breed with short legs and a sparse coat, Minskins face health challenges similar to Munchkins and Hairless breeds, such as muscle and heart problems due to their size and lack of fur.

Heterochromia

1000 Stardust

A rare condition where a cat has mismatched eyes, often seen as a curse in society. Cats with heterochromia face severe discrimination and may have one of their eyes removed.

Anisocoria

400 Stardust

A condition where one pupil is larger than the other, leading to eye pain, blurry vision, and light sensitivity. It may be caused by trauma or an underlying health issue.

Deaf

600 Stardust

Congenital deafness, often seen in white or mostly white cats, leads to complete or partial hearing loss. Affected cats may face challenges with balance and may use Cat Sign Language (CSL).

Partial Deafness

400 Stardust

A condition where a cat experiences partial hearing loss due to congenital causes or injury, which can affect balance and may communicate with Cat Sign Language (CSL).

Mute

600 Stardust

A condition where a cat is born mute or becomes mute due to physical damage. These cats are typically reliant on CSL to communicate with their peers.

Twisted Jaw

500 Stardust

A broken or twisted jaw caused by blunt force trauma can result in swelling, misalignment, and difficulty eating. Affected cats may need to eat soft foods due to their inability to chew.

Blind

600 Stardust

A cat born blind or injured to the point of blindness may rely on enhanced senses like smell and hearing to navigate their environment.

Partial Blindness

400 Stardust

A condition where a cat is blind in one eye, affecting their depth perception and balance. They may rely on enhanced senses like smell and hearing to navigate their environment.

Broken Spine

800 Stardust

A severe injury caused that can result in paralysis of the hind legs. This condition may lead to difficulty moving and respiratory issues if fluid accumulates in the lungs.

Missing or Broken Limbs

300 Stardust

Cats with missing or permanently damaged limbs may face challenges with mobility and balance. They may experience chronic pain or phantom limb sensations, and may struggle to keep up with their peers.

Lykoi

400 Stardust

The Lykoi breed has a unique roan pattern and partial hair loss, especially around the ears, nose, and feet. Despite their hairlessness, they do not face significant health problems, but may be sensitive to sun, heat, and cold.

Sphinx

400 Stardust

Sphinx cats are naturally hairless. Due to their lack of fur, they require regular upkeep of their ears, skin, and paws to prevent infection. They are prone to heart disease and muscle weakness.
