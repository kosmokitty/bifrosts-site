Activity Checks

Activity Checks (ACs) is an important system in Bifrosts, which keeps our RP community active, involved, and engaged, Bifrosts holds yearly activity checks in the form of written or drawn submissions. Overtime, inactivity can lead to stagnation, which makes it harder for active members to interact and develop their character's stories. By holding yearly activity checks we:

  • Keep the RP experience exciting and interactive and ensure that active members are able to get interactions with other characters.
  • Ensure that characters aren't sitting unused indefinitely.

What Happens If I Don't Do An AC?

If a member does not meet the activity requirement on January 1st, they will be removed from the group and their characters will pass away from sickness the next day to ensure Bifrosts holds an active roster. However, Bifrosts Staff understands that life happens! Please make a ticket if you need an extension for your activity check.

What Are The Requirements?

The same as a Graduation Assessment! Which is:

Art Requirement

  • Must be worth at least 90 Stardust.

Literature Requirement

  • Must be at least 1,350 words.
