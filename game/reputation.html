Reputation

Bifrosts runs on a reputation system—which displays how a character is treated or view by their city. Reputation affects your Charisma, and is directly tied to how convincing you'll be in your city, based on the reputation you've earned.

All characters start off at 50 reputation, which results in a +0 modifier for Charisma rolls. Reputation can be earned or lost through a characters actions by participating in Seasonal Prompts, Plots, Interactions, or Personal Plots.

Modifiers

Your characters reputation is directly tied to their Charisma. Below, you'll find the appropriate modifiers for each reputation amount and how it compares to your charisma. Don't worry about remembering all of these numbers as your statistics will show on your character card!

Reputation Charisma Status
100 +5 Modifier The shining example of their city. Leaders of the cities tend to have this reputation.
90 +4 Modifier Beyond upstanding citizens. Deeply respected by all of their city.
80 +3 Modifier Considered for leadership positions within their cities.
70 +2 Modifier Great reputation. Given important tasks and missions.
60 +1 Modifier Good reputation. Trusted by peers and given responsibility.
50 0 Modifier Neutral status. Treated the same as most peers.
40 -1 Modifier Typical starting reputation of cursed or ill-born cats. Some ostracizing and disrespect.
30 -2 Modifier Low reputation. Often distrusted or avoided.
20 -3 Modifier Extremely poor status. Must often fend for themselves.
10 -4 Modifier Severely disrespected and barely tolerated.
0 -5 Modifier Hybrids & prisoners. One wrong move risks exile or death.
