Statistics

Each player's character will have five different statistics: Strength, Constitution, Dexterity, Wisdom, and Intelligence. These six statistics determine how easy or how difficult it is to complete an action, and will translate into modifiers for action dice-rolls. These statistics will scale with a characters age and learning progression in this world. Charisma is no longer a player-determined statistic, and is now influenced by the character's reputation. A character's statistics will determine a character's ability to perform various tasks during events and all roleplays. There's no god-modding here!

Statistic Types

Strength (STR)Used for physical power and athletics.
Constitution (CON)Used for endurance and athletics.
Dexterity (DEX)Used for acrobatics, agility, sleight of hand, and stealth.
Intelligence (INT)Used for reasoning, memory, history, nature, and religion.
Wisdom (WIS)Used for perception, insight, handling, medicine, and awareness.
Charisma (CHA)Used for deception, intimidation, performance, and persuasion.
Reputation (REP)Controls how much Charisma your character has. See the Reputation tab for more details.

Lineage Bonus

Your character's lineage determines what buffs they recieve:

🔥 A cat with red eyes will receive +2 DEX.
🌊 A cat with blue eyes will receive +2 DEX.
⚡ A cat with grey eyes will receive +2 CON.
🌲 A cat with green eyes will receive +2 CON.
🌈 A cat with heterochromia will receive +4 WIS.

Base Statistics

As characters grow older, they naturally gain additional statistics. At each milestone, players can decide how to distribute these points to suit their character’s development.

Each age group has a set Statistic Point (SP) limit that cannot be exceeded. However, both city bonuses and class bonuses do not count toward this total.
