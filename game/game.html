Rules and Regulations

Below you'll find all our rules and regulations. If you have any questions, please ask in help or in a ticket!

I. General Rules

Age Restrictions

Bifrosts is an 18+ group due to heavy, recurring themes throughout the story. But we do allow one six-month exception. However, lying about your age will result in a permanent ban.

Sensitive Topics

Political topics and views must be kept out of the group. Debatable or opinionated topics tend to create resentment and arguments, and we don't wish to create that sort of environment here.

The following topics are not allowed: eating disorders, self-harm, physical abuse, sexual abuse, giving birth, and suicide. Posts including these will be deleted and the poster warned.

The following topics must be content tagged and spoilered: blood, gore, needles, trypophobia, arachnophobia, images of teeth or eyeballs, eye-straining images, strobing/flashing images, and vomiting. Please add a content tag at the beginning of the post. Spoilers are done using || on both sides.

Menstruation and pregnancy must be content tagged, but do not require spoilers.

Artwork

Please do not post over others in the artwork channels! We want to create a supportive, creative environment. React and comment on the piece above first, and ensure at least one hour has passed before posting your own work.

Drama

This is a healthy, thriving community. Do not gossip or discuss drama. If you have an issue with someone, please speak to a staff member. Never dog-pile, exclude others, or be disrespectful. Treat others how you would like to be treated!

Expected Behavior

All members and visitors are expected to be kind, courteous, and inclusive. We have zero tolerance for bullying, slurs, harassment, or rudeness. Please open a ticket if any issues arise.

Conversations about Bifrosts outside the main Discord may still be moderated to ensure members are not harmed privately.

The team reserves the right to remove anyone for behavior deemed disrespectful or unsafe.

DMing Staff Members

Please use the ticket system for complaints or issues, and do not DM staff unless given permission. Tickets include the whole team, but we strive to be kind and compromising! We will never dogpile a member, you'll speak to one staff member at a time.

Mini-Modding

Mini-modding is not allowed. Staff will answer questions and enforce rules. You may help members if they ask you directly or if they're your buddy, but do not correct or enforce rules yourself.

Buddy System

In Bifrosts, we have a buddy system! If you're new to the group, you'll automatically be assigned a 'buddy' to help familiarize you with our rules, regulations, and lore. If you'd like to be a mentor, sign up on our buddy-mentor form!

II. Character Rules

Character Age

All new-member characters must start off as kittens. This allows players to grow alongside their characters in our complex world and slowly learn the lore as their character interacts with Fyrstrheim.

Character Designs

Each city has specific palettes, markings, and eye colors, which can be viewed on their city-pages or in discord. As most of our openings are family-based, please stick to your parents designs, but feel free to ask if a design is okay in Discord — we try to be flexible.

Romance

Kittens and Novices may have innocent puppy-crushes, but their feelings should be naive and faultless. No serious romantic content should involve young characters!

You can read more about our romance rules here!

Appropriation & Conditions

If you're creating a character with a mental or physical condition, you must open a ticket first to ensures respectful and accurate portrayal. Not all conditions will have a guaranteed approval.

Becoming a City Lead

In order to become a lead in your city, your character must meet the following requirements:

  • Over 30 cycles
  • 60 reputation (+1)
  • No rule breaking or disloyal traits

They may have to wait until a city lead spot opens, so please open a ticket to see when that may be!

Cat of the Month

Each month a character is chosen via RNG. They receive +100 coins and one randomized item (single use, kept as accessory).

  • Lunar Moth Wings – reroll once
  • Colts Foot Flower – heal +10 HP
  • Arctic Fox Fangs – advantage on one roll
  • Palagonite Windchime – +2 modifier on one roll

Only once per year per player. Sick or inactive characters are ineligible; and the player must have posted 10 rumors or 1 art/lit submission that month.

Character Personality

Please do not create flawless characters. Bifrosts is morally grey — not everything is strictly good or evil. Characters should reflect their city’s culture and values, even if they differ from our own.

Nontraditional Behavior

Characters who oppose cultural values, sympathize with taboo groups, or openly defy traditions require prior ticket approval and plot purchase. Unapproved posts will be deleted to prevent unknown IC-consequences.

Mental & Physical Conditions

Psychological disorders such as mania, psychopathy, or sociopathy are not allowed. Self-harm and suicide are not permitted for characters in order to protect our member's mental health and wellbeing. All conditions require research and ticket approval.

Scars

Scars must be drawn, approved, and uploaded within one month of the event. You can get your scars approved either in a ticket or in the help channel!

III. Literature Rules

Respect Boundaries

Never control, injure, or kill another character without clear permission and staff awareness. If someone is uncomfortable, stop and compromise.

Character Interactions

In-character dislike is not out-of-character dislike. If you believe it is targeted or personal, open a ticket.

Personal Plots

We encourage character growth and personal plots. Please open a ticket to discuss any plot ideas you may have for your character — we love working with you to incorporate them!

Daily Exchange & Literature Channels

Daily-Exchange (DEs) reflect daily life and reset every 24 hours, while the channels below are often reserved as Script channels, and reset after a week. Literature channels will reset after one month of inactivity.

God-Modding & Dice

No god-modding please and keep OOC knowledge out of IC. Dice rolls are encouraged and required during public events. One roll and one post per character per round.

Character Death

Characters may die, and actions have consequences. Though, Bifrosts Staff will warn a member beforehand if a risk is severe or life-threatening. If a character dies, you may create a new one — but do not kill characters just to remake them!

IV. Activity Rules

Activity Requirements

In order to ensure that character's are not stagnant, you must write or draw a graduation assessment to allow your character to rank up from novice to adulthood! Failure to do so results in sickness. Once your character hits 25 cycles, they'll pass away due to their illness.

Leaving Policy

Please open a ticket to take a hiatus or if you're leaving the group. Leaving without notice results in character death, as we'll assume you're leaving the server for good.

V. Strike System

This group runs on a strike system. We state this now to be as transparent and honest with our members, so there are no misunderstandings and to ensure players know how the group is run before-hand.

If a rule is broken, the admin team will decide together if the user’s actions warrant a strike. Warnings may also be issued if the team believes its the best course of action. Once a decision has been made, we will open a ticket with the user and inquire about the rule breaking before giving them a strike or warning. We will never kick a member from the group without a proper explanation towards them and the other members. Bifrosts aims to be inclusive and transparent, and as such, we will not kick anyone from the group unless it is absolutely necessary.

VI. Event Rules

Consequences

Events have the potential to be lethal for your character should you choose to fight enemies. Please keep this in mind when making your character's choices as you cannot take actions back. If you actively choose to put your character in a dangerous situation when fleeing is an option, and you do not choose to have your character escape, and said character dies, you will not be able to make a new character immediately as you would if your character died through RNG and unlucky rolls trying to escape.

Rolling

You may only roll once per round after receiving approval from a staff member. If you wish to re-roll or roll with advantage, please ask to purchase the in-game item first, and then submit a claim in the claims-channel. You're allowed two roll-alterations per character per event.

If you choose to participate in an event and roll dice, in order to make your dice rolls count, you must also make a roleplay post. If you do not, your roll will be invalidated.

Posting

You can post once per round, once per character if you have multiple characters in the same event.

Disadvantage

When an event includes a fight and your character is injured below half of their full HP amount, you must roll with disadvantage for all rolls for that character for as long as your character is below half health. If a healer heals your character above half HP during the event, you will no longer be required to roll with disadvantage.

Volunteer Events

Some events are volunteer events, of which being included in these events are the result of you choosing to enter your character into an RNG pool or otherwise to be chosen to participate. Please keep in mind Volunteer Events may have different rules and guidelines when compared to Main Events, and what goes in a Volunteer Event may not go in a Main Event and vice versa.

Hiatus

If you're on hiatus during a main event, your character will be immediately removed to ensure they're safe from enemies and disasters. However, if you go on hiatus during a Volunteer Event, your character will not be immediately removed unless you ask. If you do not request a character removal, your character will remain active in the event and will be included in disasters, group rolls, and enemy attacks.

Event variation

Not all events are ran the same. It is up to the team's discretion per event to decide what rolls, posts, and choices are allowed to be made. What dice roll passes in one Main Event may be a failure in another, and what is allowed in one Main Event may not be allowed in another. Please keep this in mind when you are participating in Main Events that historical rules and guidelines in prior events may not always apply to the event you're currently in. When in doubt, always ask the associated team member who is running the event if you can do something or not.
