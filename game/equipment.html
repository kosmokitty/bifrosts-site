Equipment

Weaponry is categorized into three tiers: Light, Medium, and Specialty. Members may upgrade or exchange their equipment at any time by paying the difference in Stardust. If a purchase results in a lower-tier item, the difference will be refunded.

Example: Upgrading from a Light weapon (300sd) to a Medium weapon (400sd) requires an additional 100 Stardust.

To maintain balance in combat, a character's carrying capacity is determined by their age and experience bracket:

  • One Healing Salve

  • Light Weapon + 2 Healing Items
