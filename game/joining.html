Joining Bifrosts

Read through the Website

This website will give you a good idea on what this roleplay group is about, as well as how to shape your character! We especially recommend checking out each city, which has cultural information available to you, as well as what colors are accepted. Pay special attention to that detail, as eye-colors are restricted in this group for plot reasons.

Eye color guidelines must stay in the general range of color; saturation may change, brightness may change, but hue may not. Pelt color guidelines do not have to be exact; just ensure the colors are similar and follow the general guide.

Creating your Character

Take a look at the character creation page and the shop to make sure that you’re following our design guides! For this group, we limit specific bans, such as heterochromia, and color mutations. If you're confused on what is allowed and what's not, don't hesitate to reach out in a ticket or the help channel. Make sure to take into consideration our naming requirements. Acceptable names are of North Germanic Origin.

If the group is closed...

We will no longer be accepting applicants when the group is closed. The reasoning for this may vary, but we typically wait for enough interest and enough of a time-gap between plots for new-members to become immersed in the world. However, that doesn't mean it's impossible to join!

Kitten Tryouts

Most of the time, members will open their litter-tryouts to non-members! Just apply using the rules above, and send it into the parents. If you’re accepted, we’ll send you an invite. Bifrosts staff have no control in choosing who wins during kitten-tryouts, and it is entirely player run.

Special Openings

On special occasions, Bifrosts may hold unique openings. These openings will consist of traveler characters, pre-made characters, or adult characters being accepted into the group.

Group Opening

We shouldn't be closed for long, and we typically re-open whenever there's enough space in a city. Bifrosts staff will always inform members on our DeviantArt and in our Discord whenever there is a new opening.

Sponsorships

You could also be sponsored into the group as an orphaned character! If three people from the group have a friend they’d like to join they can submit a query regarding that. However, certain requirements must be met:

  • Members desiring to sponsor must have been in the group for one full month.
  • Sponsors cannot sponsor another person until a full month has passed.
  • The three members must open a ticket to appeal to the staff team, attesting to the applicant's personality.

The non-member desiring to join via sponsorships will then open a ticket once they have found sponsors. They will include a short application about themselves and their character. Once approved, they will discuss with the staff team how to seamlessly integrate their character.

Tryout template

Please make sure to include all required information before trying out! Incomplete biographies and applications will not be accepted. Multiple characters may be submitted if preferred.

Leaving Bifrosts

If you feel like you cannot be a part of the group anymore, please make a ticket to inform the group so we can edit our documentation and let us know what you’d like your character’s fate to be. If a member fails to inform the team, the team may choose what to do with said character.

Rejoining Policy

If you leave and want to rejoin in the future with your past character, you may! However, there are three requirements:

  1. Your character has to have been NPCd.
  2. Your character has to be living.
  3. 15 days must have passed since you left.

After one month of leaving, we may end your character at any time to keep the pace of the group.

The Fresh Start

Once a player rejoins with a previous character, their player database is wiped. They will not have any coins or shop-purchased items. However, any bans or scars are kept for consistency. Rejoined members may not resubmit past artwork.

Note: A past member is only allowed to rejoin this way once. If they try to rejoin a second time using this method, they will be treated as a new arrival. This ensures there is little confusion with players leaving and rejoining.
